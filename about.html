ABOUT US

The Foundation's Purpose

AfriTechNovative foundation is an educational non-governmental organisation created to bridge the technology gap in the African continent, through raising science and tech-conscious leaders from scratch(primary and secondary schools), in line with the sustainable development goal “4” and all, as declared by the United Nations.

Mission

Our mission is to increase the number of tech-savvy and financially-independent minds by the year 2030, in line with the United Nations sustainable development goals(SDGs).

Vision

At AfriTechNovative foundation, our vision is to make Africa a technology hub, where ideas are conceived, birthed [sic], and developed to solve the unique problems of our region with a view to finding lasting solutions and improving our standard of living.

Our Core Values

At the core of our values as a foundation are hard work,integrity and a reward system that seeks to recognize and encourage exceptional talents that distinguish themselves in learning and character, by ensuring that we do everything within our reach to assist these ones reach their full potential through instant gifts,grants and scholarships

More About Us

Who we Are

AfriTechNovative foundation is an educational non-governmental organisation created to bridge the technology gap in the African continent, through raising science and tech-conscious leaders from scratch(primary and secondary schools), in line with the sustainable development goal “4” and all, as declared by the United Nations.

Being woken to the reality that these goals cannot be achieved through the efforts of the organs and tiers of governments in our region alone, AfriTechNovative Foundation,has risen to the challenge of assisting the various nations in Africa realize these very important goals, which adversely affects our existence as a people.

This is done through various programs such as CodeMo and CodeMo STEM school clubs designed to teach and increase tech (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics) awareness amongst kids, pupils and students of various primary and secondary schools across the African continent as the foundation for the technological development of Africa, followed by a series of campaigns and scholarship interventions for primary and secondary schools pupils and students in Africa.

The main objective of this foundation is to achieve these goals which will in-turn impact positively in our socio-economic growth as a nation and Africa at large.

What We Do

We create a platform for pupils and students to become tech-breed and self-reliant through our systematic uniquely-developed strategy for exposing science, technology,engineering and mathematics to these young minds, thereby aiding their use of these strategies in practical applications to solve real life challenges and, in creating their livelihood.

Focus Area

Our focus as a foundation, is the four areas of science, technology,engineering and mathematics (S.T.E.M)
